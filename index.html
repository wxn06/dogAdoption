About

A non-profit dog rescue organization that rescues dogs who are abandoned, abused, stray, or owners who have no choice but to give away their dogs. Goal is to rescue and rehabilitate these dogs until they are adopted into their permanent homes. not only do we help dogs, we also help find the right dog for future owners.
